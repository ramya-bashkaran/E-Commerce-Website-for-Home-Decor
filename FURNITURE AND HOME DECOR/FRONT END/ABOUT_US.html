About Us

As per our name Borahae, we mean to create fabulous works for customers that they "Love it till the end of days", since purple (violet) is the last color of the rainbow. A name that resonates with the quality of workmanship and creativity that we deliver ! An innovator in the interior design industry for the last three decades, we have designed lakhs of houses for our customers, whose word-of-mouth is our major marketing force and their homes, are a standing testimony to our craftsmanship.

With the expert interor-designers we offer solution ranging from Modular Kitchen & its Accessories, Wardrobes & Lofts, TV Panel, Crockery, Bedroom n a lot more, you will never have to look beyond us for any of your interior designing requirements. The products which we sell is our own-made with excellent designers and workman.

We have our own high-tech manufacturing unit equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and technology to give our products a superior finish, which also sets us apart from our peers. Moreover, our architect’s eye-for-detail in finalizing your dream home’s interior plan and our unbelievale timeliness and quality commitment will put all your apprehensions to rest. We also partner with some of the best-in-industry product makers (Hettich, Bosh, LG, Nagold, Whirlpool, Samsung etc) to deliver nothing short of a majestic finish to all our projects.With us your interior designer, quality is not just a guarantee, but it also becomes your way of living!


Our Journey

Since 1990, Borahae has been in the business of making homes beautiful. What started with a single store in Seoul, South Korea is today the region’s preferred home shopping destination committed to constantly delivering outstanding value, continuous innovation and exceptional customer experience.

We offers an extensive and impressive range of furniture, home furnishings, home accessories, bed & bath and kitchenware. Borahae’s in-house team of stylists and buyers draw inspiration from global trends to bring stylish and affordable furniture and home furnishing to a wide spectrum of homes from traditional to modern.

In 2000, we built up our company headquarters in Itaewon-dong, Seoul, South Korea. Then we started to provide interior designing ideas to our customers.Borahae has consistently raked among the top 50 brands in the East Asia and Southeast Asia Global Brand Simplicity Index. Borahae has also been conferred the Superbrand status for three years in a row in the USA (2015-16), Japan (2016) and in Norway (2017) as well.
